What to Do with This Waste?

PET plastic is commonly used in mineral water bottles, cooking oil, or food packaging. It is transparent, lightweight, and durable.

Characteristic

• Sampah organik dapat dikompos menjadi pupuk • Pisahkan dari sampah jenis lainnya • Dapat digunakan untuk budidaya tanaman

Environmental Impact

PET plastic is recyclable, but if not disposed of properly, it can contribute to environmental pollution. It can take hundreds of years to decompose in landfills.
